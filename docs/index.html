About me

My name is Jan Stolarek and I am a Research Associate at Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science, University of Edinburgh, UK. I am on sabbatical leave from Institute of Information Technology, Łódź University of Technology, Poland. My research focuses on functional programming and compiler construction, but my scientific interests range to other areas of computer science.
